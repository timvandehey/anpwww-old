Premier Frames

Precision mitered from an extrusion of premium alloy in three professional styles - Beveled, Radiused, and Boxed - makes this sign system a top of the line favorite. Each style has several features that make professional signs simple.

  • Each frame is designed for complete support of 1/16" sign material.
  • Eight rich anodized colors are available in all three styles: Polished Silver, Satin Silver, Polished Yellow Gold, Satin Yellow Gold, Polished Rose Gold, Satin Rose Gold, Matte Black, and Arch Bronze.
  • Minimum size 2" x 4". Maximum size 36" x 36”
  • A trough 1/2" wide runs the entire inside perimeter of each frame which accommodates adhesive-backed 3M DUAL LOCK™ or Sign Snap so that a tamper-resistant plate snaps in and out with the use of a suction cup.
  • Frames are designed for mounting to wall or door with double-sided tape or silicone-type adhesive.

PREMIER PLUS FRAMES

Precision mitered from an extrusion of premium alloy in four professional styles: Beveled, Quarter Round, Boxed, and Quarter Inch Solid, makes this sign system a top of the line favorite.

  • Eight rich anodized colors are available in all four styles: Polished Silver, Satin Silver, Polished Yellow Gold, Satin Yellow Gold, Polished Rose Gold, Satin Rose Gold, Arch Bronze, and Matte Black.
  • Seven powder coat finishes are available: Medium Gray, Pewter (light gray), Black, White, Almond, Brown, and Arch Brown.
  • Each frame style is available with either a 3/16" or 5/16" inside depth. Inside depth must be specified when ordering.
  • Frames are designed for mounting with double-sided tape or silicone adhesives.
  • All frames are available with optional matte black metal inside, suitable for changeable magnetic signs.
  • Lenses are laser cut from semi-flexible .080 non glare acrylic and are designed with tabs that snap securely into frames. Lenses can easily be removed by using a suction cup placed in the center of the frame.

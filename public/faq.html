F.A.Q.
  • Where are you located?

    We are located on the south side of Waukesha Wisconsin, which is about 30 miles west of Milwaukee.


  • What artwork files do you accept?

    Since we serve such a wide range of customers, the information that we use to fabricate orders varies greatly. We have a lot of experience working directly with customers and can work from just about anything.

    Our lead-times vary based on the current production schedule and customer need. Please don't hesitate to make your needs known when ordering. Our schedule is always flexible enough to meet your needs.

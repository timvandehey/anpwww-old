Decorative Bars

Three sizes of decorative bars create bold metallic borders for your signs. Formats of top and bottom or left side, right sides are available. One can use equal size bars or mix two different sizes. All three sizes can be made in to a complete four-sided frame, and all formats can snap in a matte clear lens.

  • Bars are available in five anodized finishes: Polished Yellow Gold, Polished Rose Gold, Matte Black, Polished Silver, and Satin Silver.
  • All three sizes can be made into a complete four sided frame.
  • All formats accommodate a snap-in matte clear lens.
  • All parts are anodized individually to give finished ends.

Curves


What happens when you combine the simple easy to use aspects of a traditional holder with the flexible nature of custom frame lines? You get Curves! Innovative and cost effective.

Curves adds a whole new dimension to sign frames. Available in 27 standard sizes and 9 anodized colors, Curves has many applications including personnel signs, room identification and direction way finding signs. Curves can be mounted to a wall or partition, hung from the ceiling with fixed bracket or wire, flag mounted or used as a desk sign holder ...CURVES... literally, the options end only with your imagination!


FEATURES

  • 27 Standard Sizes
  • 9 Anodized Color Choices
  • Tamper Resistant
  • Custom Sizing Available
  • Uses 1/16" Engraving Material
  • Can use a Lens with paper insert
TYPICAL USES
  • Personnel Signs
  • Room Identification
  • Point of Purchase
  • Directional Ceiling and Wall Signage
  • Directories

Euro Sign Frames

The Euro Sign System with 20 standard sizes in 5 anodized colors is designed to make tamper resistant signs and directories. The clean lines of both the 3/8" round and 3/8" square extrusions make an attractive, functional, inexpensive solution to interior signage.

  • Five anodized finishes: Satin Silver, Polished Silver, Matte Black, Polished Yellow Gold, and Polished Rose Gold.
  • Specially designed machine screws lock the entire group of sign assemblies in place. Simply remove the top screws and slide sign assemblies out for a quick change. Screw holes are tapped for repeated use.
  • Vertical dividers can be used to form multiple columns.
  • Custom sizes are available.
